    Ingredients

    • 1 cup of flour
    • 2 eggs
    • 1/2 cup of sugar
    • 1 tsp of baking powder
    • 1/4 cup of milk

    Measurements

    • 1 cup = 240 ml
    • 1/2 cup = 120 ml
    • 1 tsp = 5 ml
    • 1/4 cup = 60 ml

    Instructions

    Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). In a bowl, mix the flour, sugar, and baking powder. Add the eggs and milk, and stir until smooth. Pour the mixture into a greased baking dish. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown.
